- English Analysis:
- The political drama surrounding Anthropic has taken a dramatic turn as President Trump indicated that a Pentagon deal is "possible." This follows a high-stakes meeting at the White House last Friday, where Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei presented the capabilities of his "Mythos" AI model to a powerful audience: Chief of Staff Susie Wiles and Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent. The presence of Bessent is particularly telling, as the Treasury oversees CFIUS (Committee on Foreign Investment in the United States), the body that likely scrutinized Anthropic’s past foreign investment ties, which initially led to the company being blacklisted as a national security risk.
- The swift transition from being a blacklisted entity to a potential defense contractor underscores the volatile nature of AI geopolitics. Previously, the administration expressed deep concerns about the dual-use risks of Anthropic's technology and its potential for leakage to foreign adversaries. However, the recent meeting suggests that Amodei has successfully pivoted Anthropic’s messaging. By framing "Mythos" not just as a "safe" model, but as a superior "strategic asset" for national defense—specifically in cyber-warfare and autonomous logistics—Anthropic has managed to align its corporate interests with the "America First" AI dominance strategy.
- The role of Susie Wiles and Scott Bessent cannot be overstated. Wiles is known for her pragmatic, results-oriented management of the President's agenda, while Bessent is a hawk on economic security. Their involvement suggests that the administration is prioritizing domestic technological supremacy over regulatory caution. If Anthropic can secure a major Pentagon contract, it would represent a massive validation, effectively neutralizing the safety-related "fear-based marketing" critiques leveled by competitors like Sam Altman. It would also position Anthropic as the premier "Constitutional AI" partner for the military—a model that is inherently more controllable and predictable for high-stakes defense applications.
- The legal limbo surrounding the Pentagon’s earlier blacklisting remains a hurdle, but the President’s comments to CNBC suggest that a policy reversal is imminent. As AI becomes the central theater of modern warfare, the companies that secure these government contracts will hold a dominant position in the global industry. This potential deal indicates that the US government is consolidating its support behind a few key domestic champions to ensure that the next generation of military intelligence is built on American soil, regardless of past regulatory friction.

- ## Mythos: 안보의 핵심 자산이 될 것인가
- 이번 태세 전환의 중심에는 앤스로픽의 최신 모델 'Mythos'가 있습니다. 앤스로픽은 이 모델이 단순한 상업용 도구를 넘어, 국가 방위와 사이버 안보에 필수적인 '전략적 자산'임을 설득하는 데 성공했습니다. 앤스로픽이 추구해온 '안전한 AI' 담론이 국방부의 엄격한 통제 요구 조건과 맞아떨어진 결과입니다. 이는 앤스로픽이 OpenAI와 차별화되는 강력한 B2G(정부 간 거래) 경쟁력을 확보했음을 의미합니다.
- ## AI 패권 경쟁과 국가 정책의 유동성
- 정부의 이러한 급격한 정책 변화는 AI 기술이 국가 경쟁력에 미치는 영향력이 그만큼 크다는 것을 반증합니다. 미국 정부는 규제나 과거의 리스크보다는 '자국 기업의 기술적 우위 확보'를 최우선 순위에 두고 있습니다. 앤스로픽과의 계약 가능성은 미국이 AI 패권 전쟁에서 승리하기 위해 수단과 방법을 가리지 않겠다는 신호탄입니다. 향후 국방 및 안보 관련 AI 시장에서 앤스로픽이 OpenAI를 제치고 독보적인 위치를 차지할 수 있을지 전 세계가 주목하고 있습니다.
