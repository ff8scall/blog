The in-person conference represents the core networking hub and the location for high-profile, physical keynotes. The simultaneous livestreaming of keynotes and sessions ensures that content reaches a global developer audience regardless of geographical constraints. This strategy is indicative of Google’s commitment to maintaining a maximally inclusive developer community. ### 4.2. Hybrid Model Efficiency
