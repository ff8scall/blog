The AI landscape shifted significantly when Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei entered the West Wing for high-stakes discussions with White House Chief of Staff Susie Wiles. Anthropic’s decision to proactively engage the Treasury Department and White House signals a new era of transparency, where the threshold for AI capability triggers national security oversight. This move highlights the growing tension between rapid innovation and the imperative to prevent a "black swan" event triggered by rogue or overly capable AI agents.
