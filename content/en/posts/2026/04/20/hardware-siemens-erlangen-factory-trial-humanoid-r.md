## Executive Summary
- A collaboration between Siemens, NVIDIA, and Humanoid has successfully trialed the HMND 01 Alpha, a wheeled humanoid robot that achieved a 90% success rate in live logistics.

## Strategic Deep-Dive

A major milestone in industrial automation was reached at the Siemens electronics factory in Erlangen, Germany. In collaboration with NVIDIA and the startup Humanoid, Siemens deployed the HMND 01 Alpha, an AI-powered wheeled humanoid, into live logistics operations. The robot completed a full eight-hour shift, mirroring a human workday.

Technical metrics were impressive: the robot averaged 60 moves per hour with a pick-and-place success rate exceeding 90%. Integrated directly into Siemens’ existing logistics software, the trial demonstrated that humanoid robots can handle complex tote-handling tasks autonomously in a dynamic environment, paving the way for wider adoption in manufacturing.
