# Tech Report: System Requirements for Counter-Strike 2 (CS2) on PC and Mac

## Introduction
Counter-Strike 2 (CS2) has generated significant interest within the gaming community as it transitions to the Source 2 engine, implementing notable graphical enhancements. Understanding the system requirements for CS2 is crucial for players seeking to optimize their gaming experience. This report synthesizes information regarding CS2's requirements from a reliable source, Hardware Times.

## System Requirements Overview
CS2's requirements are designed to accommodate the improvements that Source 2 brings, including enhanced lighting effects and intricate volumetric smoke. This necessitates a more capable hardware setup than previous iterations of Counter-Strike.

### Minimum Requirements
For players who wish to run CS2 with basic graphical settings, the following minimum specifications are recommended:
- **Operating System**: Windows 10 or higher for PC; macOS Mojave or later for Mac users.
- **Processor (CPU)**: Dual-core processor with a clock speed of at least 2.8 GHz.
- **Memory (RAM)**: At least 8 GB.
- **Graphics Card (GPU)**: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 or AMD Radeon HD 7800, or equivalent with DirectX 11 support.
- **Storage**: Minimum 15 GB of available hard drive space.

These specifications allow players to engage with CS2 but may limit the visual quality and smoothness of gameplay, particularly in graphically intensive scenarios.

### Recommended Requirements
To enjoy CS2 with optimal performance and enhanced graphics, the following recommended specifications should be met:
- **Operating System**: Windows 10 or higher; macOS Catalina or later for Mac users.
- **Processor (CPU)**: Quad-core processor with a clock speed of 3.0 GHz or higher.
- **Memory (RAM)**: A minimum of 16 GB.
- **Graphics Card (GPU)**: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 970 or AMD Radeon RX 400 series, or equivalent with DirectX 11 or better.
- **Storage**: SSD with at least 20 GB of available space for improved loading times and performance.

Meeting these requirements will ensure a smoother gaming experience, providing better graphics and frame rates, especially in multiplayer environments where visual clarity and performance are critical.

## Conclusion
With the release of Counter-Strike 2, players need to be mindful of the new system requirements driven by the switch to the Source 2 engine. Understanding both the minimum and recommended specifications will help gamers ensure their systems are prepared for the enhanced graphical fidelity and performance optimizations that CS2 offers. Preparing accordingly will allow players to immerse themselves fully in the latest installment of this iconic franchise.
