## Strategic Deep-Dive

### Empowering User Sovereignty Through 'Upgrade Ratio Option'

AMD's newly introduced 'Frame Generation Upgrade Ratio Option' presents a significant advantage compared to NVIDIA's DLSS 3. Unlike NVIDIA's approach, which supports frame generation at a fixed ratio, AMD allows users to fine-tune the frame generation ratio to match their specific system specifications and visual preferences. This empowers users to directly control the balance between frame rate improvement and input lag, enabling a more refined gaming experience.
