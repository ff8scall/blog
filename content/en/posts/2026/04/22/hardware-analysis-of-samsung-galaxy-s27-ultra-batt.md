## Strategic Deep-Dive

### Power Infrastructure Preparedness for the On-Device AI Era

Leaked data suggests the battery capacity of the S27 Ultra will range from 5,500mAh to a maximum of 6,700mAh. This represents a capacity increase of up to 30% compared to its predecessor, an essential foundation for prolonged on-device AI computation, especially for tasks leveraging power-intensive, high-performance NPU-based operations. The high energy density characteristic of silicon-carbon provides internal space, securing design flexibility for the future incorporation of more complex cooling solutions or improved camera modules.
