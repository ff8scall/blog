## Strategic Deep-Dive

### Achieving Autonomy Through an 'Always-On' Foundation

The Solode Neo's most significant differentiator lies in its 'Always-On' capability. Unlike conventional AI services that passively respond to user requests, Solode Neo supports autonomous AI agents that operate continuously in the background within a local server environment. This achieves both the security of local edge computing, where data is not transmitted to external servers, and real-time responsiveness.

Consequently, it provides a secure environment where individual and enterprise users can confidently entrust AI tasks involving sensitive data.
