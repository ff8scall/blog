## Strategic Deep-Dive

### AI: Beyond Marketing, a Practical Tool

Some market experts criticize the integration of AI into TVs as a mere marketing ploy aimed at artificially accelerating consumer upgrade cycles within a saturated home appliance market. However, the AI technology implemented by Samsung offers tangible user benefits, such as real-time energy consumption adjustments to reduce carbon emissions and enhanced screen audio guidance for the visually impaired. The fact that the TV is evolving from a passive device for displaying images to an 'AI butler' that integrates and controls home air purifiers, lighting, and security cameras represents Samsung's powerful ecosystem competitiveness.

Ultimately, Samsung's latest strategy redefines the essence of the TV from a "viewing device" to a "managing device."
