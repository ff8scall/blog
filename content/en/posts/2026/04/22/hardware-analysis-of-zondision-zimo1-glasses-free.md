### Creator-Centric Interactive Workflow

ZIMO1, adopting a productive 27-inch form factor, directly targets professional creators and content producers. Equipped with real-time interaction capabilities, users can immediately verify three-dimensional results while performing 3D modeling or design tasks. Beyond the closed hardware supply, John Deere envisions an open ecosystem, aiming to establish an end-to-end solution that empowers creators to directly generate and distribute light field content without a separate conversion process.
