## Strategic Deep-Dive

### MSI and ASUS ROG: The Emergence of Custom Xboxes

The participation of MSI and ASUS ROG, leaders in the gaming hardware sector, signals a significant diversification of Xbox hardware. Consumers will be able to choose from high-end Xboxes equipped with ASUS ROG's powerful cooling technology, or consoles featuring MSI's distinctive and stylish design, in addition to Microsoft's standard devices. This strengthens the Xbox's identity as a "living room gaming PC" while simultaneously fostering performance equalization across the entire ecosystem through competition between hardware brands.
