## Strategic Deep-Dive

### Turner's Test: The Marriage of Hardware and AI

Turner, a key figure in driving hardware innovation for Macs and iPhones, now faces the challenge of demonstrating how to seamlessly integrate unique AI experiences into next-generation devices, such as foldable iPhones and smart glasses. Whether a simple "late bloomer's excellence" strategy will suffice in the age of AI remains uncertain. If Turner fails to summon the ghost of Jobs to revitalize product essence and bridge the AI gap, Apple risks becoming merely a hardware assembly firm.
