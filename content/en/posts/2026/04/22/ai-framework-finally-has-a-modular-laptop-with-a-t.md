## Strategic Deep-Dive

### Expanding Technological Self-Determination and Market Shifts

Traditionally, major manufacturers have justified securing batteries and displays with strong adhesives by citing product slimming and integrated design. However, Framework's recent endeavor demonstrates that even core interfaces like displays can be robustly implemented with standardized connectors and screw structures. This serves as an emblematic example of how far consumers' "technological self-determination" can extend.

Framework's growth will exert real pressure on the product design methodologies of behemoths like Apple and Dell in the future, further increasing the likelihood that modular hardware will transcend its niche status and establish itself as a mainstream alternative.
