### Data Sovereignty and the Convergence of Corporate Ethics

Data transactions based on investment relationships, in particular, represent a critical legal risk that companies must be most wary of moving forward. This incident, arising from the capitalization of user trust, underscores that data governance in the AI era transcends technical concerns and has become an ethical and legal imperative for survival. Korean companies that fail to reassess the legality of previously collected data risk falling into unforeseen "retroactive data regulation" traps when venturing into the global market.
