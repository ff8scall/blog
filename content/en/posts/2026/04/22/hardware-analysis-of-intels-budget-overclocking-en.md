## Strategic Deep-Dive

### Resurgence of the Mainstream Enthusiast Market

The crux of this policy lies in the principle that "overclocking is no longer the exclusive domain of affluent users." Now, users building cost-effective systems based on i3 or i5 processors can significantly enhance system performance through advanced cooling solutions and precise voltage adjustments. This will provide a powerful incentive for purchases in the previously stagnant mid-range and budget-friendly PC assembly market, and will serve as a catalyst for motherboard and cooling solution manufacturers to aggressively launch high-performance peripherals targeted at the mainstream market.
