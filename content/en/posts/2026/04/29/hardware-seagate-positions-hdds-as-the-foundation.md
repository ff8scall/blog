## Strategic Deep-Dive

## The Infinite Loop: Why Agentic AI Needs Massive Spinning Disk Storage

Seagate Technology is challenging the narrative of the "SSD-only" future by identifying a powerful new ally: Agentic AI. Unlike generative models that respond to user prompts, agentic AI systems operate autonomously 24/7, managing complex business processes and interacting with other AI agents. This continuous operation creates an unprecedented stream of unstructured data.

From detailed audit logs to intermediate state captures, the volume of machine-generated data is projected to dwarf human-generated content within the next 24 months.

## TCO Metrics and the Reality of Data Gravity

As these autonomous agents generate more data, "Data Gravity"—the concept that large data sets attract more applications and services—comes into play. Organizations cannot afford to host these massive, ever-expanding data lakes on expensive flash storage. Seagate’s analysis indicates that the Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) for high-capacity HDDs remains the most viable economic path for storing unstructured AI data.

While SSDs dominate the 'hot' layer of active inference, HDDs are becoming the essential 'warm' and 'cold' layers where the collective memory of agentic workflows is preserved. By focusing on cost-per-terabyte and reliability at scale, Seagate is positioning its latest high-capacity drives as the indispensable foundation of the AI era. As agentic AI becomes a standard corporate tool, the demand for mass-capacity storage is seeing a significant and sustained structural rebound.
