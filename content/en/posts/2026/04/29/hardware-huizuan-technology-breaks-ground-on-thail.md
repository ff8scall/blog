## Strategic Deep-Dive

Huizuan Technology, the entity expanding from Superior Plating Technology's specialized manufacturing expertise, has officially broken ground on its new facility in Ayutthaya, Thailand. With an initial investment of NT$ 300 million (approximately US$ 9.52 million), the company aims to transform the site into a high-end hub for optical communications and storage technologies. The strategic move to Thailand aligns with the broader industry trend of diversifying manufacturing footprints away from traditional hubs to mitigate geopolitical risks and tap into Southeast Asia's growing tech ecosystem.

The facility is specifically designed to scale the production of Co-Packaged Optics (CPO) and high-density AI-optimized hard drives (HDD). As AI training clusters demand higher data throughput and more efficient thermal management, Huizuan’s focus on CPO and advanced cooling solutions positions it as a critical tier-2 supplier for the global AI infrastructure. The integration of high-end plating technologies—a core competency of the parent company—will be utilized to enhance the durability and conductivity of these specialized components.

Slated for completion in late 2026, the Ayutthaya plant will serve as a lighthouse facility, demonstrating how high-precision manufacturing can scale to meet the rigorous demands of next-generation AI data centers.
