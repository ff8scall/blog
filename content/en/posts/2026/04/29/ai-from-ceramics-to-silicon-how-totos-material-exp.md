## Strategic Deep-Dive

## Engineering Precision: Ceramics in the Lithography Stack

Toto is redefining its industrial identity by applying its core ceramic expertise to the semiconductor equipment sector. The company's advanced materials are increasingly used in the production of Electrostatic Chucks (ESCs) and precision wafer handling components, which are vital for maintaining structural integrity under the extreme thermal and chemical stress of EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) lithography.

## Supporting the Next-Gen AI Compute

As the demand for high-density AI accelerators grows, the tolerances for chip fabrication equipment have tightened significantly. Toto’s ability to manipulate ceramic properties at a molecular level provides a critical solution for semiconductor equipment manufacturers struggling with thermal stability. This strategic pivot illustrates how deep-tech material science, rooted in legacy manufacturing, is becoming an indispensable layer of the modern AI hardware infrastructure.
